Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about this project

Is my grade sheet being stored?

No, it is not. The website takes the values from your grade sheet and caches them in your browser temporarily. If you reload the page, this data will be removed. We do not store any of your information on our servers.

Where can I find the official BRACU retake and repeat policy?

This calculator is a tool to help you analyze your grades, but for the official and most up-to-date BRAC University retake and repeat policies, please visit the official BRACU website at https://www.bracu.ac.bd/academics/policies-and-procedures. Always refer to the official policies for accurate information about retake eligibility, procedures, and academic regulations.

What is the purpose of this website?

As a BRAC University student, I always had to maintain an Excel sheet to see how much my CGPA would increase if I retook a course, or to determine what GPA I needed to achieve my dream CGPA. This was quite a hassle, so I created this website to simplify the process.

Can I see the source code?

Absolutely! Here is the source code. If you think of any features, just create an issue. Even better if you can implement it and make a pull request. Also, please give it a star if you can—it will help me out a lot!

Can I download what I created here?

Yes, you can download your data in Excel format and make changes to it as needed. You can also upload your modified Excel file to restore your data.

What is the simple version?

The simple version does the same thing as this website—calculating your future CGPA—but it's more straightforward and user-friendly. You don't need to upload your grade sheet; just provide your current CGPA and credit-related information, and you're good to go. It's perfect for quick calculations when you don't have your grade sheet.
